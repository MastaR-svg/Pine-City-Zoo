MO'S PIZZA

Mo's Pizza is a popular spot for visitors to enjoy delicious pizza and refreshing drinks. The menu features a variety of pizzas, including vegetarian options, and a selection of beverages to quench your thirst.

Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a place to relax with friends and family, Mo's Pizza provides a casual and friendly atmosphere. It's the perfect place to refuel during your visit to the zoo.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

Mo's Pizza is conveniently located near the main entrance of the zoo, making it easily accessible for visitors. The restaurant features both indoor and outdoor seating, allowing you to enjoy your meal while taking in the sights and sounds of the zoo.
