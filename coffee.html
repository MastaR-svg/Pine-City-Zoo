WILD THINGS COFFEE SHOP

Relax and enjoy a drink at the Wild Things Coffee Shop. This cozy spot offers a variety of beverages, including coffee, tea, and smoothies, as well as light snacks and pastries.

Whether you're looking for a quick pick-me-up or a place to unwind, the coffee shop provides a comfortable atmosphere with seating both indoors and outdoors. It's the perfect place to take a break during your visit to the zoo.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

The Wild Things Coffee Shop is conveniently located near the entrance of the zoo, making it easily accessible for visitors. It is designed to provide a relaxing environment where you can enjoy your refreshments while taking in the sights and sounds of the zoo.
