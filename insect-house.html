INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House is a fascinating place to explore the world of insects. It features a variety of exhibits showcasing different species of insects, their habitats, and their roles in the ecosystem.

Visitors can learn about the life cycles of insects, their behaviors, and their importance in pollination and decomposition. The Insect House also provides an opportunity to observe live insects in a controlled environment.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

The Insect House is located near the entrance of the zoo, making it easily accessible for visitors. It is designed to provide a comfortable and educational experience for all ages.
