MONKEY TRAIL

The Monkey Trail is an exciting and interactive experience that allows visitors to walk through a specially designed enclosure where monkeys roam freely. This unique trail provides an up-close view of various monkey species in a naturalistic setting.

As you stroll along the trail, you can observe the monkeys' playful behavior, social interactions, and agility as they swing from branches and climb structures. The trail is designed to educate visitors about the different species of monkeys, their habitats, and their conservation status.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

The Monkey Trail is located in the heart of the zoo, surrounded by lush vegetation and naturalistic habitats. It is designed to provide a safe and immersive experience for visitors while ensuring the well-being of the monkeys.
