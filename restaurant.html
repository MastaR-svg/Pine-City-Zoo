DINE @ THE ZOO

Relax and enjoy a meal at the Dine @ The Zoo restaurant. It offers a variety of dishes made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients. The restaurant features both indoor and outdoor seating, providing a comfortable dining experience for all visitors.

Whether you're looking for a quick snack or a full meal, Dine @ The Zoo has something for everyone. The menu includes vegetarian and vegan options, ensuring that all dietary preferences are catered to.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

Dine @ The Zoo is conveniently located near the main entrance of the zoo, making it easily accessible for visitors. The restaurant is designed to provide a relaxing atmosphere where you can enjoy your meal while taking in the sights and sounds of the zoo.
