AMPHITHEATRE

The amphitheatre is a large open-air venue that hosts various events, including animal shows, educational talks, and special performances. It provides a unique opportunity for visitors to learn about the animals in an engaging and entertaining way.

The amphitheatre is designed to accommodate a large audience, with tiered seating that allows everyone to have a good view of the stage. The shows often feature trained animals and informative presentations by zookeepers.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

The amphitheatre is located in the heart of the zoo, easily accessible from all major pathways. It is surrounded by lush gardens and provides a beautiful backdrop for the shows.
