LOST FOREST

The Lost Forest is a unique and enchanting area within the zoo, designed to mimic a natural rainforest environment. It features lush vegetation, winding paths, and a variety of animal exhibits that showcase the diverse wildlife found in tropical forests.

Visitors can explore the Lost Forest on foot, taking in the sights and sounds of the rainforest. The area is home to various species of birds, reptiles, and mammals, all of which are adapted to life in a dense forest habitat. Educational signage throughout the area provides information about the animals and their ecosystems.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

The Lost Forest is located in a secluded part of the zoo, surrounded by tall trees and natural landscaping. It is designed to provide a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of the zoo, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the beauty of nature.

Don't forget to bring your camera to capture the stunning scenery and unique wildlife!
